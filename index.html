Our Process

01

Walk the space

Measure rooms, check subfloors, talk through materials, and flag anything that affects schedule or cost.

02

Plan the details

Set the layout, transitions, removal needs, prep work, and timing so install day is predictable.

03

Install cleanly

Protect the home, keep the work area tidy, and focus on straight lines, stable seams, and careful cuts.

04

Review together

Walk the finished floor, answer care questions, and make sure the room is ready for real life again.
